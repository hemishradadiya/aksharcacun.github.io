Our Culture

Our culture is woven with the values of trust, respect, integrity, innovation, sustainability, and collaboration. We nurture an environment that inspires creativity, fosters continuous learning, and instills a deep sense of responsibility toward our mission. At the heart of our culture lies innovation. We believe in challenging the status quo and are constantly seeking new and improved ways to enhance the quality and sustainability of our cocoons and silk products. Our teams are encouraged to think creatively, explore unconventional ideas, and embrace cutting-edge technologies to craft superior solutions.


Sustainability is a cornerstone of our organizational culture. We are dedicated to conducting our business in an environmentally responsible manner, ensuring that our processes not only meet the needs of today but also preserve the resources and beauty of our planet for future generations.

Our Values

Environmental Sustainability We are dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability through our practices. We focus on preserving natural resources, reducing waste, and minimizing environmental impact in every step of our manufacturing process.

The health, safety, and well-being of our employees, customers, and the communities we serve are of utmost importance. We maintain a safe and healthy work environment while adhering to strict safety and environmental standards.

Innovation We foster innovation to continuously improve our technologies, processes, and products. Our commitment to innovation helps us advance sustainable practices and enhance the quality of our cocoons and silk products.

Social Responsibility We are committed to being socially responsible and contributing to the well-being of our communities. We engage in initiatives that support local development and prioritize ethical practices in all aspects of our business.

Transparency and Accountability We uphold transparency in our operations, ensuring that all stakeholders are informed about our impact, goals, and progress. We take accountability for our actions and strive for continuous improvement.

Collaboration and Partnerships We value collaboration with like-minded organizations, government bodies, and communities. By working together, we aim to achieve a more sustainable and prosperous future for the cocoon industry.
